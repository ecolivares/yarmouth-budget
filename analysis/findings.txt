5. PROPERTY-TAX MECHANICS (FY27)
==============================================================================
   Net taxable value (tax base): $3,221,025,900
   Tax rate: $15.25 per $1,000
   Standard example home: $500,000  →  annual tax $7,625
   *** CONVERSION FACTOR ***
   Every $1,000,000 cut from appropriations lowers:
      • the tax rate by $0.310 per $1,000
      • the bill on a $500k home by $155/year
   NOTE: the FY25→FY26 rate drop ($25.67→$14.55) is a REVALUATION, not a
   cut — the tax base ~doubled. Compare the LEVY ($ raised), not the rate.
