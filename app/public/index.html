How a cut becomes a tax change

⚠️ Watch the levy, not the rate.

The tax rate fell from $25.67 to $14.55 between FY25 and FY26 — that was a revaluation (the town's taxable value roughly doubled), not a tax cut. The amount actually raised from property taxes (the levy) rose every single year. Always compare the levy across that boundary.
