What changed each year

The increase residents are feeling isn't one big decision — it's steady annual growth, and most of it is schools.

What you actually pay is the levy — and it climbs every year

The levy is the total dollars raised from property taxes. It has gone up every single year, even the year the rate fell.

⚠️ Don't be fooled by the rate.

The tax rate dropped from $25.67 (FY25) to $14.55 (FY26) — but that was a revaluation (the town's total assessed value roughly doubled), not a tax cut. Your assessment jumped too. Watch the levy, not the rate.
